I'm Shree — a second-year mechanical engineering student at VIT Pune, building at the intersection of simulation, ML, and physical design. My work sits in the space between a CAD file and a finished analysis: FEA, CFD, teardown methodology, and increasingly, software that automates the pipeline between them.

Currently deep in CAE Research — teardown documentation, NVH modelling, and powertrain vibration simulation. Outside academia, I'm developing ForgeIQ, a web platform that converts STEP/SolidWorks files into manufacturing cost estimates and production reports using AI.

Long-term targets: Formula 1, aerospace structures, or defence systems — wherever simulation-driven design is pushing limits.
